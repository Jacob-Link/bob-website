4. Information We Collect

4.1 Selected Text Data

When you invoke Bob, the text you highlight (“Selected Text”) is transmitted to our backend API to generate an AI explanation. Selected Text may be stored in the future for the sole purpose of improving Bob user experience.

4.2 Preference Data

Bob stores one setting - your preferred explanation tone - on your chrome storage. This data helps to adjust the response returned by Bob to the users liking.

4.3 Technical & Usage Data

We create a non-reversible hash of your IP address and retain it for up to 24 hours to enforce fair-use limits. We do not collect fingerprints, User-Agent strings, or browsing history.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Performance of a contract – to deliver the requested explanation (Art 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Legitimate interests – to protect service integrity by rate-limiting abuse (Art 6(1)(f) GDPR).

6. Purpose of Processing

We process data exclusively to (i) deliver on-page explanations, (ii) remember your chosen tone, and (iii) prevent service abuse.

7. Data Retention

Selected Text is stored to improve future UX. IP entries used for the rate limits auto-expire after 24 hours. Local preference data remains on your device as long as Bob is installed.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share data with cloud-hosting providers under strict DPAs; processors act solely on our instructions and implement appropriate safeguards.

9. International Transfers

Our servers are in North America. If you access Bob from elsewhere, your data may be transferred internationally. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

10. Security Measures

All traffic is encrypted. We employ network isolation, least-privilege access controls, and regular audits.

11. Your Rights

12. Children’s Privacy
